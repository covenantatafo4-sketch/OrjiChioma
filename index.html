Words From My Soul
Written only for you, Lovelyn
Before you, I wandered in the quiet dark, A hollow vessel searching for a spark — Then you arrived like dawn upon the sea, And suddenly, the world made sense to me.
You are the warmth that melts the coldest night, The gentle hand that guides me toward the light. You are my calm when chaos finds my door, You are my Antidote — and so much more.
As Aphrodite rises from the foam, So you arose and made this heart your home. A goddess wrapped in grace, in laugh, in light — Chioma, meaning "good God" — how right.
I love the way your laughter fills the air, The softness of your skin, the crown you wear, The way your eyes hold stars I cannot name, The way you whisper — never twice the same.
You are the poem I could never write, The prayer I breathe at the edge of night, The masterpiece that needs no frame, no wall — You, Lovelyn, are the finest gift of all.
They speak of love as fleeting, light as air, But what I feel is mountains — beyond compare. Rooted, raging, rising like the tide, A love so fierce it swells and cannot hide.
You are the antidote to all my pain, The rainbow living on the other side of rain, My Aphrodite, born of love's own sea — There is no me without a you in me.
So here I lay my heart, completely bare, In every song, in every whispered prayer — Through every sunrise and the midnight blue, Orji Lovelyn Chioma — I choose you.
— With every heartbeat, forever yours 💜
